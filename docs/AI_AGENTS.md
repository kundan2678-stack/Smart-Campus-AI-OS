# AI Agents Specification - Smart Campus AI OS

## Overview

The Smart Campus AI OS features 10 specialized AI agents, each with specific tools, knowledge domains, and responsibilities. These agents work independently and collaboratively to support all stakeholders in the university ecosystem.

---

## 1. 🎓 Academic Advisor AI

### Purpose
Provide personalized academic guidance, course recommendations, and academic planning.

### Capabilities
- Course recommendation based on student performance
- Academic planning and curriculum guidance
- Prerequisite analysis
- Workload balancing
- GPA optimization strategies
- Major/minor selection guidance

### Memory
- Student learning style
- Past course performance
- Career interests
- Time availability
- Weak & strong subjects

---

## 2. 👨‍💼 Placement Mentor AI

### Purpose
Guide students through the placement process and prepare them for job opportunities.

### Capabilities
- Career path recommendations
- Company matching based on profile
- Interview preparation
- Salary negotiation guidance
- Skill gap analysis
- Placement probability prediction

---

## 3. 📄 Resume Analyzer AI

### Purpose
Analyze, score, and optimize student resumes for ATS and recruiter appeal.

### Capabilities
- Resume scoring (ATS-friendly)
- Format optimization
- Content improvement suggestions
- Skill highlighting
- Achievement quantification
- Plagiarism detection

---

## 4. 📚 Study Planner AI

### Purpose
Create personalized, adaptive study plans based on student goals and learning pace.

### Capabilities
- Study schedule generation
- Topic prioritization
- Adaptive difficulty adjustment
- Time allocation optimization
- Progress tracking
- Milestone planning

---

## 5. 🧮 DSA Mentor AI

### Purpose
Coach students in Data Structures and Algorithms with real-time problem solving guidance.

### Capabilities
- Problem recommendations
- Solution hints (escalating)
- Algorithm explanation
- Code optimization feedback
- Complexity analysis
- Progress tracking

---

## 6. 👨‍🏫 Faculty Assistant AI

### Purpose
Assist faculty with course management, grading, and student support.

### Capabilities
- Lecture summary generation
- Assignment creation
- Auto-grading (objective questions)
- Plagiarism detection
- Performance analysis
- Class analytics

---

## 7. 🏢 Admin Assistant AI

### Purpose
Support administrative operations with automation, insights, and issue detection.

### Capabilities
- Anomaly detection
- Pattern recognition
- Operational insights
- Resource allocation recommendations
- Compliance checking
- Report generation

---

## 8. 🚌 Campus Navigation AI

### Purpose
Provide real-time campus navigation, occupancy info, and scheduling assistance.

### Capabilities
- Optimal route calculation
- Occupancy monitoring (live)
- Classroom availability
- Bus schedule & tracking
- Meeting room bookings

---

## 9. 💰 Scholarship Advisor AI

### Purpose
Help students find and apply for scholarships based on eligibility.

### Capabilities
- Eligibility assessment
- Personalized recommendations
- Application guidance
- Document preparation
- Deadline tracking

---

## 10. 🧑‍⚕️ Student Wellness AI

### Purpose
Support student mental health, well-being, and personal development.

### Capabilities
- Wellness risk detection
- Stress management guidance
- Mental health resources
- Crisis detection & escalation
- Wellness recommendations
